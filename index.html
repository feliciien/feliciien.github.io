06 — Strategy Logic

Where the
edge lives.

01 — Liquidity

Sweep & Reversal

The strategy identifies institutional stop-hunt sequences around key structural levels. Entries are confirmed post-sweep, capturing the resulting displacement. Edge is derived from the mechanical behavior of order flow at these levels.

02 — Session

Dislocation Windows

London open and New York open produce recurring inefficiencies as institutional order flow enters. The system exploits these time-gated dislocations with session-aware entry filters and adaptive position sizing.

03 — Volatility

Compression Breakout

Abnormal range compression relative to historical ATR is used to identify pre-expansion conditions. Positions are structured asymmetrically — limited downside, uncapped upside within the day's range objective.

04 — Expectancy

Asymmetric Structure

Risk/reward ranges from 1.1 to 3.5 depending on setup quality. A 40% win rate at 2.5:1 average R:R produces positive expectancy. The system does not require directional accuracy — it requires structural discipline.

Specific entry logic, parameter values, and signal generation mechanisms are proprietary and not disclosed. This summary is provided for strategy characterization purposes only.

09 — Execution Model

No custody.
Full control.

Execution

Fully Automated EAs

Custom-built Expert Advisors in MQL5. Zero discretionary override at the trade level. Rules are encoded and executed algorithmically — no human interpretation required at entry or exit.

Custody Model

Investor-Controlled Accounts

Investor capital is never transferred to BotIQ Capital. The strategy is replicated directly onto accounts controlled by the investor. No commingling of funds. No custody risk.

Copy Trading Structure

Signal Replication

Trades executed on the master account are replicated to investor sub-accounts in real time. Each investor account maintains independent capital and can be disconnected at any point.

Risk Infrastructure

Automated Circuit Breakers

A dedicated Risk Manager EA operates independently on all accounts. It enforces daily loss limits and triggers an automatic hard stop if the portfolio drawdown reaches the pre-defined threshold.

Liquidity

No Lock-Up Periods

Investor capital remains fully liquid at all times. There are no redemption gates, lock-up structures, or withdrawal restrictions. The investor retains full control of their account.

Reporting

Transparent Monitoring

Real-time account access for all investors. Monthly performance summaries provided. FTMO public profile available for independent verification at any time.
